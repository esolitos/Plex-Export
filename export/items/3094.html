Bajatey Raho is a 2013 Hindi Bollywood comedy film directed by Shashant A Shah and produced by Krishika Lulla. The film features Tusshar Kapoor, Dolly Ahluwalia, Ranvir Shorey, Vinay Pathak and Vishakha Singh in lead roles. The film revolves around the life of the family of one Mr. Baweja, who was falsely accused of a bank fraud, who apparently dies due to this, and how his family take matters into their own hands and plan revenge on an businessman Sabbarwahl who had framed him in the first place, and pay back all the innocent people whom Sabbarwahl has duped. Bajatey Raho is director Shashant Shah's third film, after Dasvidaniya (2008) and Chalo Dilli (2011), both of which starred actor Vinay Pathak as lead. The film released on 26th July 2013, and received mixed response from critics.
