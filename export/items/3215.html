Prisoners is a 2013 American mystery thriller film directed by Denis Villeneuve. The film has an ensemble cast including Hugh Jackman, Jake Gyllenhaal, Viola Davis, Maria Bello, Terrence Howard, with Melissa Leo, and Paul Dano. The plot focuses on the abduction of two young girls in Pennsylvania and the search to find them. The film is nominated for an Academy Award for Best Cinematography.
