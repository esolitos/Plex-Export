The Last Ride is a 2011 American drama about the last days of country music pioneer and legend Hank Williams. The film stars Henry Thomas, Jesse James, and Fred Dalton Thompson, and received a limited release on October 21, 2011. Despite the content, no songs by Williams are ever played during the film. The soundtrack is composed of covers of Williams' songs among other country musicians.
