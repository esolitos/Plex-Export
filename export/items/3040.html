Cronos is a 1993 Mexican vampire horror film written and directed by Guillermo del Toro, and starring veteran Argentinean actor Federico Luppi and American actor Ron Perlman. Cronos is del Toro's first feature film, and the first of several films on which he collaborated with Luppi and Perlman.
