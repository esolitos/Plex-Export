Jai Ho is a 2014 Indian Hindi action drama film directed by Sohail Khan and produced by Sohail Khan along with Sunil Lulla, starring Salman Khan, Tabu and newcomer Daisy Shah in major roles along with Suniel Shetty (cameo) and Genelia D'Souza (cameo). The film has Tabu playing Salman's sister while Danny Denzongpa plays the main antagonist.The basic premises of the plot has been inspired from Pay it Forward and Stalin. Box Office India declared the film a semi-hit in India.
