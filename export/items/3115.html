Comedy

Grown Ups 2 is a 2013 American buddy comedy film directed by Dennis Dugan, and also produced by Adam Sandler, who also starred in the film. The film is the sequel to the 2010's Grown Ups. The film co-stars Kevin James, Chris Rock, David Spade, Nick Swardson, Salma Hayek and Steve Austin. Rob Schneider did not reprise his role from the first film because of his scheduling conflicts. This was Steve Austin's first theatrical release film since 2010's The Expendables. The film is produced by Adam Sandler's production company Happy Madison and distributed by Columbia Pictures. The film was released on July 12, 2013, and was heavily panned by critics.
