Khiladi 786 is a Bollywood action comedy film directed by Ashish R Mohan, featuring Akshay Kumar in the title role alongside Asin Thottumkal playing the female lead. The film also features Mithun Chakraborty, Raj Babbar, Mukesh Rishi and Himesh Reshammiya in supporting roles. The film marks the return of Akshay Kumar to his Khiladi film series after 12 years. The film is mostly shot in Mumbai and Punjab. It released on 7 December 2012, and after a slow start, Box Office India said that it had taken 61.50 crore nett in its first two weeks.
