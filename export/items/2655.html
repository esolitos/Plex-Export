The Impossible is a 2012 English-language Spanish disaster drama film directed by Juan Antonio Bayona. It was written by Sergio G. Sánchez, stars Naomi Watts and Ewan McGregor, and is based on the true story of María Belón and her family's experience of the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami. The film received positive reviews from critics for its direction and its acting, especially for Watts who has been nominated for an upcoming Academy Award for Best Actress and who was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Motion Picture Drama.
