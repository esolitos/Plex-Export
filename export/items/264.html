National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation 2: Cousin Eddie's Island Adventure is an American made-for-TV comedy movie. It premiered December 20, 2003 on NBC. It is the sequel to National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation, although it is more of a spin-off than a direct chapter in the Vacation series of movies, because the main stars (Chevy Chase and Beverly D'Angelo) from the original films do not appear. It stars Randy Quaid and Miriam Flynn, reprising their roles as Cousin Eddie and Catherine. This was the last Vacation series film to-date to be linked to the National Lampoon name, although Warner Bros. still holds the trademark for the franchise name "National Lampoon's Vacation" and its characters.
