You're Next is a 2011 American horror film directed and edited by Adam Wingard, written by Simon Barrett and starring Barrett, Sharni Vinson, Nicholas Tucci, Wendy Glenn, A. J. Bowen and Joe Swanberg. The film had its world premiere at the 2011 Toronto International Film Festival Midnight Madness program. The film was released on August 23, 2013 in the United States to positive reviews and has so far grossed over $26 million at the box office, surpassing its budget of $1 million.
