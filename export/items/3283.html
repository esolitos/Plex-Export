One Chance is a 2013 British comedy film about Britain's Got Talent winner Paul Potts, directed by David Frankel and written by Justin Zackham. It was screened in the Special Presentation section at the 2013 Toronto International Film Festival. The film was shot in Italy and the United Kingdom. The soundtrack is scored by Theodore Shapiro and was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song - motion picture.
