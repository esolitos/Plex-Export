Main Hoon Shahid Afridi is a Pakistani action-drama sports film directed by Syed Ali Raza Usama and produced by Humayun Saeed & Shahzad Nasib. The film features Humayun Saeed, Javed Shaikh and Noman Habib in the lead roles. Nadeem Baig, Shafqat Cheema, Ismail Tara, Ainy Jaffri, Hamza Ali Abbasi and Shahzad Sheikh also play important roles in the film, whilst Shahid Afridi and Ayesha Omar make special appearances.
