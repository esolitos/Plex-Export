Thriller | Crime Fiction | Drama

The Iceman is a 2012 American crime thriller film directed by Ariel Vromen based on the life of notorious Mafia hitman Richard Kuklinski. It was released in 2012 at the Venice Film Festival and stars Michael Shannon, Winona Ryder, Chris Evans and Ray Liotta. The Iceman screened at the 2012 Telluride Film Festival and the 2012 Toronto International Film Festival before receiving a limited release in cinemas in the United States on May 3, 2013. It expanded into more cinemas in the USA on May 17.
