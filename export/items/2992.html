Crazy, Stupid, Love. is a 2011 romantic comedy-drama film directed by Glenn Ficarra and John Requa, and written by Dan Fogelman. It stars Steve Carell, Ryan Gosling, Julianne Moore and Emma Stone along with Analeigh Tipton, Jonah Bobo, Marisa Tomei and Kevin Bacon. The film was released in United States and Canada by Warner Bros. Pictures on July 29, 2011. Upon release, the film received positive reviews, earning a 78% approval rating and a "Certified Fresh" rating at Rotten Tomatoes. The film was also a box office success, grossing $19,104,303 in its opening weekend and earning a total of more than $140 million, far exceeding its $50 million budget.
