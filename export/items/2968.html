The Perks of Being a Wallflower is a 2012 American coming-of-age film and is a film adaptation of the 1999 epistolary novel of the same name. The film stars Logan Lerman, Emma Watson, and Ezra Miller, and was directed by the novel's author, Stephen Chbosky. Filming in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, started on May 9 and ended on June 29, 2011. The film was released on September 21, 2012 to positive critical response, grossing almost $29 million worldwide.
