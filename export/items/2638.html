Sleepwalk with Me is a 2012 American independent comedy film written by, directed by, and starring comedian Mike Birbiglia. The film premiered at the 2012 Sundance Film Festival and won the Best of NEXT Audience Award. It was produced by Jacob Jaffke and This American Lifes Ira Glass in association with Bedrocket Entertainment and WBEZ Chicago.
