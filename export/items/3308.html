Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story is a 1993 American semi biographical martial arts film telling the story of actor and martial arts expert Bruce Lee, starring Jason Scott Lee as Bruce Lee, Lauren Holly as Linda Emery, and Robert Wagner. The film was directed by Rob Cohen and written by Cohen, Edward Khmara, and John Raffo. Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story was dedicated to Bruce and his son Brandon Lee, who was accidentally killed two months earlier while filming The Crow.
