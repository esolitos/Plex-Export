Underworld: Evolution (also known as Underworld 2) is an action-horror film directed by Len Wiseman. It is the second (chronologically, the third) installment in the Underworld series, released Underworld in 2003. The events of the film begin during the same night of the first film's finale. In the film Selene and Michael fight to protect the future of the Corvinus bloodline from its hidden past.
